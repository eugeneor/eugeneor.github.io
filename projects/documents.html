Project Iris

January to September 2014 @ Clio

Problem

Clio does not lead the document management area of the legal sector. To stay competitive, we need to build a product that will specialize in documents and tailored to the needs of attorney and solicitor.

Solution

Design a stand alone web application that will allow lawyers to create and upload files/folders. We created collaboration tools that would let attorneys share their files, create versions, and make comments. Permissions, full search, and filter capabilities were also enabled at the get go.
