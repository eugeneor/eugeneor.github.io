Onboarding Tour

November 2012 to April 2013 @ Clio

Problem

Clio handles anything from client intake to billing. The workflow may be confusing for some of the less tech savvy personas. The goal is to educate as well as drive conversions.

Solution

Design an on boarding flow that will teach customers about the Clio workflow. Using a step by step process, we walked customers through the each of the key pieces that make up Clio. Upon completion of the tour, we would grant them a referral credit. The tour should be expandable to include other feature tours based on customer interest.

Success Metrics

  • 80% found the on boarding tour successful
  • 65% maintained interested throughout completion
  • 64% had a pretty good user experience, 32% thought it was awesome
  • 30% said the tour had an affect on their conversion
